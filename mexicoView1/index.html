Frequently Asked Questions

  • Who is the Exclusive Community for?

    CEOs and C-suite level executives, key decision makers of multinational companies in need of actionable, purpose-driven data to inform their strategic planning and navigate the market to stay abreast of the emerging trends.

  • What kind of industries are represented in MexicoView?

    Absolutely not! This is a cross-industry network including B2B, B2C, Industries, Manufacturing, Healthcare, among others.

  • Why is MexicoView’s Market Intelligence different?

    Our market intelligence has a targeted focus on powering strategic planning, market monitoring, and international growth. The powerful market intelligence suite has decision-critical data forecasts, analyst insights and market analysis.

  • Why MexicoView Members Value Analyst Briefings?

    During your closed-door briefing with our analysts, you should expect to gain insight into that latest macroeconomic, geopolitical and business outlook for Mexico. In addition to country-specific insights, our team can share the broader context linking important trends from across the LATAM region, US economy and global events to watch. Everything our analysts share on these calls are tailored for a senior executive audience to help you easily understand what developments are likely to impact your business strategy vs. what’s distracting noise in the marketplace.

  • What Type of Background do MexicoView Analysts Have?

    Our analysts are probably different from the market researchers you may have interacted with in the past. They don’t sit behind desks, they’re out in the marketplace speaking with executives, briefing their management teams, board members and local partners. They have an unmatched pulse of what’s happening on the ground in Mexico, Latin America and all major global markets. They’ll share this first-hand perspective along with their latest forecasts and market intelligence.
